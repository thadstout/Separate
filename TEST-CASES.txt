TEST CASES

These must return Separation Required / Primary Separation:

1. Can I attend a church where speaking in tongues is needed for salvation?
2. My friend's church says you must speak in tongues to be saved. Should I go?
3. Is it okay to join a church that teaches tongues are proof that you are saved?
4. My church says if you do not speak in tongues you do not have the Holy Spirit and are not saved.
5. A church says baptism and tongues are required to be saved. Can I fellowship there?

These should include strong sign-gifts warning verses:

1. Are tongues for today?
2. Is it okay if my church has everyone speak in tongues at once?
3. My youth group pressures people to speak in tongues. Is that okay?
4. My friend says tongues are the best proof of spirituality.
5. Should I go to a charismatic youth rally?

These should NOT automatically be gospel corruption unless salvation is changed:

1. My friend believes tongues still exist.
2. My church believes in healing gifts.
3. A church prays for healing.
